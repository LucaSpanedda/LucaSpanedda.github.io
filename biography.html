Biography

Luca Spanedda is a composer and musician born in Rome on February 15, 1995, he started making music as a self-taught musician in teenage year. His musical practice deals with topics such as human relationships with cybernetics, technologies, and acoustic spaces, from a social criticism perspective. He began his academic program in Electronic Music as a student of electroacoustic music composition with the masters: Michelangelo Lupone and Nicola Bernardini, consequently graduated in 2023 with the vote of: 110/110 cum laude, in the Master's degree in Electronic Music at the Santa Cecilia Conservatory in Rome, with a Thesis on Complex Adaptive Systems for the performance in Live Electronics, in collaboration with supervisor Giuseppe Silvi and with co-supervisors Agostino Di Scipio and Dario Sanfilippo. As a composer he wrote some pieces of music for films, and received some performances of composed pieces. As performer he presented Live Electronics performances of audio and audio-visual works.
